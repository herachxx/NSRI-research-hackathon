Privacy Policy

How NSRI handles hackathon data.

This page explains what the National Student Research Institute collects for the Summer Research Hackathon, how we use LinkedIn API and comment data, and how to contact us.

Last updated

July 5, 2026

Contact

nsrinstitution@gmail.com

What NSRI Collects

NSRI collects information needed to run the hackathon, support participants, and review submissions. This may include:

  • Registration details such as name, school, role, country or region, track interest, team information, and contact email.
  • Event participation information such as attendance, team assignments, support requests, submission files, project links, presentation materials, and judging scorecards.
  • Payment or ticket status from third-party payment or registration tools. NSRI does not intentionally collect full payment card details on this website.
  • Basic website and communications data, such as browser/device information, form responses, email interactions, and analytics used to keep the event running reliably.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate registration, form teams, send event updates, provide participant support, host judging, deliver feedback, announce winners, prevent abuse, improve future NSRI programs, and meet legal or operational obligations.

LinkedIn API and Comment Data

If NSRI uses LinkedIn API features or LinkedIn comment-based workflows for hackathon outreach, partner recognition, verification, or engagement tracking, we may process LinkedIn-visible information that a user has made available through LinkedIn or authorized through the relevant LinkedIn flow. This may include public profile identifiers, names, profile links, organization names, comment text, post identifiers, timestamps, and engagement metadata.

NSRI uses this LinkedIn data only for event-related administration, verification, analytics, communications, partner reporting, and community engagement. We do not use LinkedIn comment data to make unrelated eligibility decisions, and we do not collect private LinkedIn messages through this website.

Data Sharing and Selling

NSRI does not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers, event platforms, judges, mentors, partner organizations, or operational volunteers when needed to run the hackathon, review submissions, provide support, or publish event results. These uses are limited to legitimate event purposes.

Retention and Security

NSRI keeps event information for as long as reasonably needed for hackathon operations, participant support, reporting, nonprofit records, and future program improvement. We use reasonable organizational and technical safeguards, but no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You may contact NSRI to request access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to event integrity, recordkeeping, and legal requirements. For privacy questions, email nsrinstitution@gmail.com.
